Bachelor in marketing & Fullstack developer student

I’m 26, born and raiser from Karlstad, and currently wrapping up my fullstack development studies. Before that, I earned a degree in digital marketing, and I’ve done internships in both Stockholm and Copenhagen. Outside of work, I value balance. I enjoy working out, catching up with friends and family, and watching (and suffering with) Manchester United.

I’m the kind of person who’s curious, thrives when there’s a mix of structure and creativity, and who always tries to bring good energy. To challenge myself and learn new things has always been important to me, and I've always strived to step outside my comfort zone.

What do I love with marketing and web development?

What I love about marketing is the creative puzzle – understanding people, shaping messages, and creating content that actually connects. Fullstack development, on the other hand, gives me the satisfaction of building something from the ground up and making sure every piece fits – from logic and data to design and flow.

Combining the two has helped me see the full picture. I’m not just thinking about how something looks or functions, but how it supports the user at every step of their journey. Whether it’s a campaign, a landing page, or a full product – I like to understand the why behind the what.
